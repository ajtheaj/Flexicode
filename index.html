About
FlexiCode

As the demand for online learning platforms grows, FlexiCode is the solution for IT education. In FlexiCode, the learners are anticipated to enhance their strong and weak learning styles in order to excel in their programming classes. Not only that, this platform was developed to promote a continuous, interactive, and fun learning towards students who finds the traditional way of learning boring. Together with FlexiCode, let us engage in the world of programming with challenge and fun.
